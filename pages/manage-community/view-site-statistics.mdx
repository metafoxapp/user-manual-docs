# View site statistics

### In this post, we show you where you can take a closer look at the traffic stats you can quickly view on your website, the numbers that show how your site is doing. 

  

-   Select the **Dashboard** tab in Admin CP. Here you'll see a overview of the general information needed to know. 

![image2022-12-13_16-47-34.png](/images/image2022-12-13_16-47-34.png)

  

-   You can find your total number of **users, blogs, pages**..... depending on the type of app you choose, you can also view the statistics graph in **Daily** and **Weekly**.

![image2022-12-13_17-2-34.png](/images/image2022-12-13_17-2-34.png)

 ![image2022-12-13_17-3-1.png](/images/image2022-12-13_17-3-1.png)

  

On the right, you'll see the **in-depth stats** of your site including the **Today Statistics** and **Site Statistics.** Click on **the View more** button for more apps to view.

![image2022-12-13_17-7-17.png](/images/image2022-12-13_17-7-17.png) ![image2022-12-13_17-8-3.png](/images/image2022-12-13_17-8-3.png)

  

Scrolling down a little bit, you find the following information:

-   Latest ACP Logins:  
-   New and Updates:  Admin can stay updated on new blogs here
-   Site Status: here you can find the status of your license, the expiration of your support plan, and the site version.
