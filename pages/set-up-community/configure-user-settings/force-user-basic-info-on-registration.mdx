# Force user basic info on registration

This guide will walk you through the process of forcing users to provide basic information during registration on your phpFox V5 platform. This can help you collect essential data about your users and enhance their experience on your site.
## Steps to Force User Basic Info on Registration
1. Log in to the Admin Control Panel (AdminCP) with your administrator account.
2. Navigate to: **Members** > **Custom Fields** > **Manage Basic Info Fields**.
3. In the list of basic info fields, locate the field you want to make mandatory during registration, enable the **Required** option and **Registration** option for that field. Changes will be saved automatically.
4. Repeat step 3 for any additional fields you want to require during registration.
5. Clear site caches to ensure the changes take effect on the frontend.


Done! You have successfully forced users to provide basic information during registration. This will help you gather important data about your users and improve their experience on your platform.
