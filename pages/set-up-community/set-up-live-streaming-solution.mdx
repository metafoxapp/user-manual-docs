# Set Up Live Streaming Solution

Live Streaming lets users instantly share their moments through live videos and connect with their audience from anywhere. Whether you want to host talk sessions, webinars, live events, or showcase your products/services, Live Streaming has got you covered. This solution comes equipped with features like notifications for new live videos, real-time reactions, comments, and stickers for the best community engagement. Expand your reach and boost your views through live videos!

## Key Features

-   Live streaming from PC or mobile apps
-   Chat and comments throughout the live streams
-   Reactions on live streams
-   Watch all the live-streaming videos again
