# Enable Subscription on Registration

In this article, we will guide you enabling subscription on Registration so your users can choose their appropriate Subscription package during registration.

  

Let's log into your MetaFox site with the Admin user. Then go to **AdminCP** > **App Settings** > **Subscription** > **Settings**

Enable the **Is a Subscription required in Registration?** setting to require members to select a subscription package when they register.

![Screenshot 2024-03-12 at 16.21.57.png](/images/Screenshot%202024-03-12%20at%2016.21.57.png)

All Subscription packages enabled for Registration and available for **Guest User** role will be listed on the Registration form

![Screenshot 2024-03-12 at 16.31.41.png](/images/Screenshot%202024-03-12%20at%2016.31.41.png)

That's it. You can enable Subscription packages on the Registration form now.
