# Live Streaming - Infrastructure Requirements

To operate the Live Streaming solution, on top of a one-time payment for this solution, you will need to pay a monthly fee for infrastructure and external services such as storage, video live encoding, and streaming.

The monthly cost is based on the following factors:

-   The monthly live streaming videos
-   Duration of an average live stream
-   Monthly live stream view count
-   How long does the average viewer watch

To control your budget more conveniently, we recommend you to reach out to Mux (https://mux.com/) to get a quote for your expected live streaming usage. 

If you have any questions for other cases, please feel free to contact us via [Ticket System](https://clients.phpfox.com/).
