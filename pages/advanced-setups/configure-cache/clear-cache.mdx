# Clear cache

This article will guide to how to clear cache on MetaFox site

Log into AdminCP of your site. Click on the **Broom** icon on the top header bar.

![Screenshot 2023-07-03 at 10.19.15.png](/images/Screenshot%202023-07-03%20at%2010.19.15.png)

Click on the **Clear Cache** button.

Done! All caches have been removed properly then.
