# Upgrade license

The assumption is that you are holding a MetaFox-Pro license and want to upgrade to the MetaFox-Ultimate license to get the Premium White-label MOBILE APP. If so, please follow the below steps:

**Step 1**: Go to [https://clients.phpfox.com/](https://clients.phpfox.com/) and log in to your account

**Step 2**: Clicking on **Services** will redirect you to the **My Products & Services** page**\>**Continue clicking on your **active MetaFox-Pro license and** choosing A**ctions**\>**Upgrade Package.**

![Upgrade.png](/images/Upgrade.png)

**Step 3**: It will take you to the **Upgrade Package** page>Please scroll down to see the button **Choose Product**

**![choose product.png](/images/choose%20product.png)**

**Step 4**: **Checkout** and complete the payment. After that, the Support team will reach out to you to convert the license and instruction some needed work.

![checkout.PNG](/images/checkout.PNG)
