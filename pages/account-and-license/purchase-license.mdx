# Purchase license

This article is going to guide you to purchase MetaFox licenses.

You can check all available MetaFox license types on the [MetaFox Landing page](https://www.metafox.app) or [place an order here](https://clients.phpfox.com/index.php?rp=/store/metafox-licenses)

  

![MetaFox licenses.png](/images/MetaFox%20licenses.png)

  

Click on **Order Now** button of the license you would like to purchase, then go to the **Review & Checkout** page

![checkout.PNG](/images/checkout.PNG)

  

In the **Review & Checkout** page, you can:

-   Verify the license you are going to purchase
-   Add Promotion code if having
-   In the Billing Details, if you don't have an account, please choose **Create a new Account** option and provide all required details
-   Stick the checkbox to agree with Terms of Service
-   Checkout to make payment via Paypal payment gateway.

Once payment is made, you will receive a confirmation email with purchase details.

Yay, we are almost done with the license purchase. 

  

If you have any difficulties with the purchasing process, you can contact us via: [https://clients.phpfox.com/](https://clients.phpfox.com/)
