# Welcome to phpFox User Manual

Welcome to the comprehensive user manual for phpFox, your complete social networking platform solution.

## What is phpFox?

phpFox is a powerful and flexible social networking platform that allows you to create and manage your own social community. With phpFox, you can build engaging online communities with features like:

- **User Profiles** - Customizable user profiles with photos, videos, and personal information
- **Activity Feeds** - Real-time updates and social interactions
- **Groups & Events** - Create and manage communities and gatherings
- **Marketplace** - Buy and sell items within your community
- **Mobile Ready** - Responsive design that works on all devices
