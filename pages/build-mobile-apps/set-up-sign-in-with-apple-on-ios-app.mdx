# Set up Sign in with Apple on iOS app

This article will guide you how to set up Apple Login with your iOS App. The setup includes 2 steps: Generate Service Key on Apple Developer and configure that key on the MetaFox site.

## Generate Service Key on Apple Developer

Now, let's log into your Apple Developer and go to the **Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles**

  

![apple 1.png](/images/apple%201.png)

Click on the  button to register a new identifier for Apple Login. In the Register page, please choose the **Service IDs** option as below

![apple2.png](/images/apple2.png)

Click **Continue** button to move the next step to fill the Identifier and Description.

The Identifier should be prefixed by the App Bundle ID. For example, if your iOS app is having bundle ID *com.metafoxapp.mobile* , you can use the identifier of *com.metafoxapp.mobile.apple1* as below

Then click the **Continue** button.

![apple3.png](/images/apple3.png)

In this step, you can select the Sign in with Apple option. Then Click on the Configure button to configure App ID

![apple5.png](/images/apple5.png)

  

In this **Configure Key** screen, you will choose the App Bundle ID of your iOS app as below. Then click the **Continue** button

![apple4.png](/images/apple4.png)

Click on the Register button to complete the registration.

![apple6.png](/images/apple6.png)

You will need to download the key as well as note the Key ID to configure in the MetaFox site.

![apple7.png](/images/apple7.png)

You have registered successfully the Identifier for Sign in with Apple on Apple Developer. Now, we will move ahead to configure the MetaFox site for the Sign in with Apple

## Configure Metafox site

Let's log into your MetaFox site with Admin user and go to the **AdminCP > App Settings > Social Connect**. Then, choose the **Apple** tab on the top header

You will configure the settings as below and then save to complete the setup.

![Screenshot 2023-05-30 at 16.54.50.png](/images/Screenshot%202023-05-30%20at%2016.54.50.png)

Congratulation. You have completed the setup for the **Sign in with Apple** for your iOS now.
