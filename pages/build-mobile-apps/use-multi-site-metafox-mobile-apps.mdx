# Use multi-site MetaFox Mobile apps

***Step 1: Install the iOS and Android app***

-   You will need to go to the **Apple Store or Google Play** on your device and search for our app named ***MetaFox,*** then proceed with downloading and installing the app.

***Step 2: Getting started***

-   After installing successfully, open the app
-   On the first screen, please enter the URL of your MetaFox site in the ***Server Address*** field. For example: if your site is at URL: *[https://yourdomain.com](https://yourdomain.com)*, please put the value "*[https://yourdomain.com](https://yourdomain.com)"* into this field.
    

                                ![320916134_1129080804347327_3801861718095174961_n.jpg](/images/320916134_1129080804347327_3801861718095174961_n.jpg)

Then tap **Connect button.**

**Step 3:** If the Mobile API app has been set up correctly on your site, users will go directly to the login Screen.

  

                              ![Capture.PNG](/images/Capture.PNG)

-   On this **Login Screen**, enter the user's login credentials on the **MetaFox** site. There are options for users to **Sign up for** a new account or reset their password and log **in with other options like Facebook and Google.**

-   After signing in/up, users can enjoy your social with the multiple-site MetaFox Mobile app.

***And finally, hope you will have a good time with our app!***
