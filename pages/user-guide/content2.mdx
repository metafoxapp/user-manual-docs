# Content Management

Master content management in phpFox to create engaging and well-organized content for your community.

## Content Types

phpFox supports various content types:

### Posts & Updates
- Status updates
- Rich text posts
- Shared content
- Polls and surveys

### Media Content
- Photos and albums
- Videos
- Audio files
- Documents

### Pages & Blogs
- Static pages
- Blog articles
- News posts
- Announcements

## Managing Posts

### Post Overview

Access all posts at **AdminCP → Content → Posts**

View and filter by:
- Post type
- Author
- Date range
- Status (published, pending, hidden)

### Featured Posts

Highlight important content by featuring posts on your homepage or category pages.

## Photo Management

Configure photo settings:
- Max file size
- Allowed formats
- Compression quality
- Watermarks

## Video Management

Handle video uploads and processing:
- Auto-conversion to MP4
- Multiple quality versions
- Thumbnail generation

## SEO Optimization

Optimize content for search engines:
- Meta titles and descriptions
- SEO-friendly URLs
- XML sitemaps
- Open Graph tags

## Content Analytics

Track content performance:
- Views and engagement
- Popular content
- User interactions
